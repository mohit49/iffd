About IFFD

The International Film Festival (IFFD) is a flagship initiative envisioned under the Delhi Film Policy, aimed at positioning Delhi as a vibrant global hub for cinema, cultural exchange, and storytelling. Conceived as an international celebration of films and filmmaking, IFFD seeks to spotlight cinematic excellence while fostering meaningful cultural and industry collaborations across borders.

Delhi Film Policy — e-Film Clearance

Delhi has, for decades, been the choice of destination for film-makers opting for locales that add an element of timeless beauty and character to their works. This ancient city has been immortalized in countless movies. The Delhi Film Policy is an initiative to promote film shooting in the city's. Delhi Tourism is the nodal agency for the facilitation of film shooting in Delhi.

The Delhi Film Policy aims at:

  • Streamlining approvals
  • Supporting creativity, talent, and tourism
  • Single-window digital approvals via ‘e-Film Clearance’
  • Transparent access for stakeholders and authorities
  • Automated communication notifications
  • Instant application acknowledgments

About DTTDC

The Delhi Tourism & Transportation Development Corporation (DTTDC) is a government agency that promotes tourism and film production in Delhi. As the nodal agency for Delhi Film Policy 2022, it streamlines film shoot approvals to attract productions.

DTTDC is organising the International Film Festival of Delhi (IFFD) in association with the Government of Delhi, with a view to elevate Delhi's global film presence and to contribute to the growth and diversification of the film and media ecosystem in the national capital.

Beyond films, DTTDC handles a wide range of city infrastructure and services. It manages attractions like Dilli Haat and the Garden of Five Senses, runs tourist centres and the HoHo bus, and organizes cultural events.
